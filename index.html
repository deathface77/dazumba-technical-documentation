Setup Instructions

1. Charging The Speaker

  1. Connect the USB-C charging cable to the charging port on the speaker.

  2. Plug the other end into a power source (wall adapter or USB port).

  3. Allow the speaker to charge fully (approximately 3 hours). The LED indicator will turn green when fully charged.

2. Pairing with a Bluetooth Device

  1. Turn on the speaker by pressing the Power button until you hear a chime.

  2. Press and hold the Bluetooth button until the LED indicator flashes blue, indicating pairing mode.

  3. On your device (phone, tablet, etc.), go to Bluetooth Settings and select X100 Speaker from the list of available devices.

  4. Once connected, the LED will turn solid blue, and a connection confirmation chime will sound.

3. Using the Aux Cable (Optional)

  1. Insert one end of the included 3.5mm aux cable into the AUX port on the speaker.
  2. Connect the other end to your audio device's headphone jack.
  3. The speaker will automatically switch to aux mode when the cable is connected.

Troubleshooting

1. Speaker Won't Turn On

  • Solution: Ensure the speaker is charged. Connect the speaker to a power source and check if the LED indicator turns on. If the LED does not light up, try a different USB cable or power adapter.

2. Bluetooth Connections Issues

  • Solution:
  • Make sure the speaker is in pairing mode (LED should flash blue).
  • Verify that Bluetooth is enabled on your device and that it's within range (10 meters).
  • If the device does not connect, try restarting both the speaker and the Bluetooth device, then attempt to reconnect

3. Poor Sound Quality or Distortion

  • Solution: Check the volume levels on both the speaker and your connected device. Distortion may occur if the volume is too high. For Bluetooth connections, ensure there are no obstructions or sources of interference between the speaker and the device.

4. Speaker Not Charging

  • Solution: Confirm the charging cable and adapter are functioning by testing with another device. If the speaker still won't charge, inspect the charging port for debris or damage

Safety Information

  • Do not submerge the speaker in water.
  • Avoid exposure to extreme heat or prolonged direct sunlight.
  • Only use the included or certified replacement charging cables.
  • Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.
